## üåü Features

### For Clients (Users)
- **Browse Professionals** - Discover photographers, videographers, and event organizers
- **Secure Booking System** - Book services with integrated payment processing
- **Review & Rating System** - Leave reviews and rate professionals
- **Booking Management** - Track your bookings and payment history
- **Real-time Notifications** - Get updates on booking status

### For Professionals (Photographers/Videographers)
- **Professional Dashboard** - Comprehensive business management interface
- **Booking Management** - Accept/decline bookings and manage schedules
- **Earnings Analytics** - Detailed revenue tracking and performance metrics
- **Portfolio Showcase** - Display your work and attract clients
- **Client Communication** - Direct messaging with clients

### Technical Features
- **Responsive Design** - Works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile
- **Modern UI/UX** - Clean interface with smooth animations
- **Authentication System** - Secure JWT-based user authentication
- **Protected Routes** - Role-based access control
- **API Integration** - RESTful API communication with backend
- **Performance Optimized** - Lazy loading and optimized rendering

## üìñ How to Use the Platform

### üÜï Getting Started

#### Step 1: Choose Your Role
1. Visit the homepage
2. Select your role:
   - **Client** - Book photography services
   - **Photographer/Videographer** - Offer your services

#### Step 2: Create Account
1. Click "Sign Up" based on your role
2. Fill in your details:
   - **For Clients**: Name, email, password
   - **For Professionals**: Name, email, password, business details
3. Verify your email (if required)

#### Step 3: Login
1. Use your credentials to log in
2. System automatically redirects to your role-specific dashboard

---

### üë§ For Clients

#### Discovering Professionals
1. **Browse Categories**
   - Navigate to "Discover" from the navbar
   - View photographers, videographers, and event organizers
   - Filter by rating, location, or specialization

2. **View Profiles**
   - Click on any professional's card
   - View their portfolio, ratings, and reviews
   - Check availability and pricing

3. **Make a Booking**
   - Select your preferred professional
   - Choose service package
   - Pick date and time
   - Add event details and special requirements
   - Confirm booking with payment

#### Managing Your Bookings
1. **View Bookings**
   - Go to "My Bookings" in the navbar
   - See upcoming and past events
   - Track booking status (Pending, Confirmed, Completed)

2. **Payment Management**
   - Navigate to "Payment" section
   - View payment history
   - Manage payment methods
   - Download receipts

#### Leaving Reviews
1. **After Event Completion**
   - Go to completed bookings
   - Click "Leave Review"
   - Rate the service (1-5 stars)
   - Write detailed feedback

---

### üì∏ For Professionals

#### Dashboard Overview
1. **Home Dashboard**
   - View earnings summary
   - See upcoming bookings
   - Track business metrics
   - Access quick actions

#### Managing Bookings
1. **Booking Requests**
   - Navigate to "Bookings" section
   - View pending requests
   - Accept or decline bookings
   - Set availability status

2. **Client Communication**
   - View client contact information
   - Message clients directly
   - Share event details and requirements

#### Tracking Earnings
1. **Analytics Dashboard**
   - Go to "Earnings" section
   - View revenue trends and charts
   - Track monthly/yearly performance
   - Export financial reports
   - Monitor client satisfaction rates

#### Managing Reviews
1. **Review Management**
   - Access "Reviews" section
   - View all client feedback
   - Respond to reviews
   - Track rating improvements

### üîí User Profile Management
